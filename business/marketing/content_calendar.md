# Content Calendar Generator

## Context
Content marketing requires consistent planning and organization.
This prompt helps create a structured content calendar for various marketing channels.

## Prompt
```
Create a detailed content calendar for [timeframe: e.g., next month, next quarter] for a [business type] focused on [industry/niche].

Target audience:
- [Describe primary audience demographics, interests, pain points]

Marketing goals:
- [List 2-3 primary marketing objectives]

Our brand voice is: [describe brand voice: professional, conversational, humorous, etc.]

Please include the following in the content calendar:
1. Topics for [list channels: blog posts, social media, email newsletters, etc.]
2. Posting frequency for each channel
3. Content themes aligned with our goals
4. Key dates and relevant industry events to leverage
5. Content types (educational, promotional, entertaining, etc.)
6. Call-to-action recommendations for each content piece

Format the calendar as a weekly or monthly schedule with specific dates and content details.
```

## Notes
- Adjust the timeframe based on your planning cycle
- For more detailed guidance, include information about competitor content strategy
- You can request content ideas be tied to specific product launches or promotions
