# Short Story Generator

## Context
Creating engaging short stories with well-developed characters, settings, and plots can be challenging. This prompt helps generate creative short fiction.

## Prompt
```
Write a short story of approximately 1000-1500 words based on the following parameters:

Genre: [specify genre - mystery, sci-fi, fantasy, romance, horror, etc.]
Setting: [specify time period and location]
Main character: [describe main character or leave for AI to create]
Theme: [central theme or message]
Mood: [emotional tone - suspenseful, whimsical, melancholic, etc.]

Additional elements to include:
- [Any specific plot elements, objects, or motifs]
- [Any specific literary techniques to employ]

The story should have a clear beginning, middle, and end with a satisfying resolution or thought-provoking conclusion.
```

## Notes
- For more experimental stories, you can request non-linear narratives or multiple perspectives
- Adding specific constraints (like "must include these three words" or "must be told entirely in dialogue") can produce interesting results
- You can request the AI to emulate a specific author's style for additional variety
